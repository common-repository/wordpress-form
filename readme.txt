Wordpress form generates a custom contact form on your website sidebar, page, or post. 

== Description ==

<p>Wordpress Form is a Wordpress plugin that generates a contact form on your website or blog. It can also be used as a suggestion box. After adjusting the preferences (such as where do you want the email to go) simply insert text [WORDPRESS-FORM] in any page, post, widget content and the form will appear.</p>

== Installation ==

Here is what you need to do: 1. Install plugin OR unzip the downloaded file to /wp-content/plugins/1. Go to your plugin settings and enable the plugin.1. In the sidebar you will have a new section called 'Wordpress Form' navigate there and set your preferences.1. Insert [WORDPRESS-FORM] shortcode in any post, page or widget.
1. If you are not recieving emails, you may need to turn off Allow Comments on the page where you place [WORDPRESS-FORM].
